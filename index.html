About me

I'm a French engineer & entrepreneur. Since 8, I'm passionated by Maths and its applications. I love finding solutions to elegantly solve complexe problems with Technology & Science.

I've been graduated by l'Ècole Centrale de Lille, France (French Engineer Grande École) in 2013 after a specialization in eCommerce and Organisations Management. During my student years, I've been freelancer, helping companies to create their digital branding.

In 2012, during an internship in Singapore, I've tech-founded Villa-Bali.com, an online plateform to rent villas in Bali. I've been CTO at Villa-Finder for 3 years: mother company of Villa-Bali.com, we created after having launched our new destinations: Phuket & Samui in Thailand and Sri Lanka.

After an internship at 3Suisses in 2013, one of the most important eCommerce in France, I realized how powerful is Data Analysis. I'm now Head of Data Science at Luxola, #1 APAC beauty eCommerce, using all my experience in sciences, maths, programming & business to help taking strategic decisions.
